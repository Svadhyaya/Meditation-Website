Whether you are a student, parent or simply someone looking for time out, here you have come to the right place.

This is your time - make sure to savour it all.

Stop what you are doing.

  • Sit comfortably, straighten your back and take a deep breath in.
  • Notice the breath as it flows in and out of your nostrils, feel your belly rise and fall.

    • The breath is your anchor to the present, whenever your mind wanders, slowly bring your attention back to the breath.

  • Close your eyes and become aware of your body. Let go of plans or worries, and let yourself ease into the moment for the next 5 minutes.

An ancient practice

With roots in Hinduism, Buddhism, Islam and Christianity one does not have to look far when contemplating meditation. This is as much a tool for reflection and focus, as it is for exploring spirituality - Just let yourself do the seeking and you shall find whatever it is you are after.
